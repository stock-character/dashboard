The PR-Stat Initiative

A Data-Driven Approach to a More Efficient & Resilient Pico Rivera

Vision Statement

The PR-Stat Initiative is a proposed city-wide program designed to leverage the data we already collect to enhance decision-making, improve operational efficiency, and increase public trust through transparency. By centralizing our key metrics into a series of interactive dashboards and analytical tools, we can move from a reactive to a proactive model of governance. This portal serves as the central hub for these new tools, providing management with at-a-glance insights into the fiscal health, performance, and long-term trajectory of our city.
